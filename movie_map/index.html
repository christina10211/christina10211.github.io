100 Greatest Movies in 21st Century: An Interactive Movie Map

BY Christina Zhang

Published August 7, 2018

BBC polled 177 critics all over the world about their favorite 10 movies in 21st century. In this project, I selected three movie genres: action, comedy, and romance, and created an interactive map based on origin conturies of movie directors with the help of mapbox and leaflet.

This is a genre analysis on the 598 movies selected by 177 critics all over the world.
