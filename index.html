Spark: San Francisco Crime Data Analysis and Modeling

The motivation and goal for this project is to help SFPD better plan their police force coverage over all the districts over the day, and also help tourists identify dangerous districts and hours to visit in SF.

During this coursework project, I conducted online analytical processing on 15-year SF crime data on Databricks using Spark SQL and Dataframes API to gain insights into crime trends from 2003 to 2018 and provide some police power, policy adjustment, and tourism advices.
