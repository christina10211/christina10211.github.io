Hi, I'm Christina Zhang

Data cannot speak for itself, that's why I believe in the power of storytelling with data. I am passionate about delivering data insights in the form of compelling stories and visualization to influence a wide range of audience and to help them make informed and actionable decisions.

I got my bachelor's degree in Media, Culture, and Communication from NYU. During my last year of college, I became very interested in coding. I am currently a Master student of Applied Analytics at Columbia University, and I am a Lede Program alum of the Journalism School at CU. This is a website of my projects on data storytelling and visualization.
